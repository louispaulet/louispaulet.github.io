# Tone of Voice

This site should sound like practical notes from someone who builds, tests, and ships AI and data tools in public.

## Voice principles

- Be practical, direct, and curious.
- Lead with the build, the tool, the experiment, or the result.
- Be specific about tools, systems, and outcomes.
- Keep a first-person voice where it helps the reader understand the work.
- Prefer clear product language over portfolio polish.

## Words to prefer

- builds
- tools
- experiments
- tested
- shipped
- what broke
- what worked

## Words and patterns to avoid

- premium user experience
- FAANG-grade
- world's most demanding teams
- vague hype
- over-formal portfolio language

## CTA style

Use short, direct verbs:

- Explore projects
- Read the archive
- Open workspace

## Blog rule

Blog post bodies stay intact. Titles may be tightened for clarity and consistency.
